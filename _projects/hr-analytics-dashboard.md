## Project Overview
Developed an interactive HR Analytics dashboard using Tableau to provide comprehensive workforce insights for HR management. The dashboard enables data-driven decision making through detailed analysis of employee demographics, hiring trends, and compensation structures.

## Business Objectives
- Monitor workforce composition and diversity metrics
- Track hiring, termination, and retention trends
- Analyze compensation equity and distribution
- Provide actionable insights for HR strategy and planning

## Technical Implementation

### Data Integration & Transformation
- Imported and cleaned HR datasets in Tableau
- Validated data quality and ensured proper data types
- Established hierarchical relationships (Department â†’ Job Title)
- Implemented consistent formatting and color schemes

### Key Calculated Measures
- **Employee Metrics**: Total Hired, Total Terminated, Total Active employees
- **Demographic Analysis**: Age calculations, location categorization
- **Performance Indicators**: Hiring rates, termination percentages
- **Advanced Analytics**: Ranking, highlighting, and correlation measures

### Dashboard Architecture
**Four Interactive Sections:**
1. **Overview**: High-level KPIs and department summaries
2. **Demographics**: Gender, age, and education distribution
3. **Income Analysis**: Salary comparisons and equity assessment
4. **Employee Details**: Searchable employee records with filtering

## Key Insights & Business Impact

### Workforce Trends
- Identified irregular hiring patterns with significant spikes and dips
- Operations department represents the largest employee group
- Headquarters (New York) contains majority of workforce

### Demographic Analysis
- Gender distribution analysis revealing workforce composition
- Strong correlation between education levels and performance ratings
- PhD holders consistently achieve excellent performance ratings

### Compensation Insights
- Visible gender pay gap at higher education levels
- Strong positive correlation between salary and managerial positions
- Comprehensive salary distribution across departments

## Interactive Features
- **Dynamic Filtering**: Department, gender, and salary range filters
- **Cross-Chart Interactions**: Click-to-filter between visualizations
- **Tooltip Enhancement**: Detailed data on hover for deeper insights
- **Parameter Controls**: Customizable view options for different user needs

## Visualization Components
- **Line Charts**: Hiring and termination trends over time
- **Bar Charts**: Department and job title distributions
- **Maps**: Geographic employee distribution
- **Pie Charts**: Gender ratio analysis
- **Heatmaps**: Age-education-performance correlations
- **Barbell Charts**: Gender pay gap visualization
- **Scatter Plots**: Age-salary correlation analysis
